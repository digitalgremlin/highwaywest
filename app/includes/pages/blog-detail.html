Summer Events In Coos Bay

June 4th, 2015

The free spirited nature of some travelers sometimes means that trips are booked impulsively and the plans are often as open as the road that leads them to their destination. For those who put the plans in the backseat and don’t plan activities ahead of time, we have some good news for you! Here are some great summer events in Coos Bay!

Our Oceanside Beachfront RV Resort in Coos Bay, OR has a number of recurring local events that may occur during your visit. No matter which days you are visiting, there is a good chance you will be able to take advantage of one of the staple activities that helps make the Oregon adventure coast so exciting.

If you happen to be in Coos Bay on a Wednesday sometime between now and October, you can enjoy farm fresh fruits, vegetables, flowers and more at the local farmers market in the heart of downtown.

The First Friday Wine Walk occurs monthly and visitors can wine taste and shop at many of the local shops in downtown Coos Bay. Grab a commemorative wine glass while you’re there!

Foreign Film Friday occurs on the second Friday of each month at the Coos Bay Public Library. Free admission and refreshments!

The South Coast Striders coordinate free hikes numerous times a month. For those unfamiliar with the area, this is a great way to navigate trails with knowledgeable locals and nature lovers. They have developed a calendar that displays the dates and times for each hike as well as useful information such as meeting points, the types of terrain hikers can expect to encounter, and information regarding parking. To view their calendar, here.

Of course, there are always unique events happening each month in Coos Bay and Oceanside is always happy to provide you with useful event and festival information as you plan your trip. Please visit oceansidervresort.com for more information regarding reservations and accommodations.
